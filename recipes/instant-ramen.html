Ingredients

  • Instant ramen packet
  • 300ml water
  • 50g green onions

Steps

  1. Add water to a saucepan, cover, and bring it to a boil. Once boiling, add the packaged seasonings.
  2. Once boiling, add the dried noodles. Set a timer for 5 minutes. Using chopsticks, submerge the dried noodles in the soup and loosen them up.
  3. Sprinkle some green onions. When the timer beeps, transfer the noodles to a bowl.
